You speak. An AI interviews you — not about your job titles, but about how you think, what drives you, how you make hard calls. At the end, you get a structured .md file — your identity in a format that AI hiring agents can actually read, reason over, and respect. A professional bio and CV come with it.

~20 minutes · no account needed · voice or text

Why this exists

Recruiters already use AI to screen candidates. The question is what that AI reads.

Right now it reads a CV — a document written for human eyes, optimised for keywords, filtered by ATS scanners. You get reduced to a skills list. The context, the judgment, the way you actually work — gone.

Eigenself creates a profile written natively for AI. Not keywords — structured reasoning patterns, decision-making context, communication style. The things a good interviewer would surface. Encoded so an AI agent on the other side can actually evaluate fit.

What you get

One interview. Three documents.

The protocol is the source of truth. The bio and CV are rendered from it — for the contexts that still need them.

02 Rendered from protocol
Professional Bio

A warm, human-readable narrative generated from your protocol. Ready to paste into LinkedIn, a portfolio, or a conference page.

A clean, structured CV for systems that still require it. Not the point — but it comes for free, and it's accurate.
